Zero Waste Cambridge is a passionate, local business serving Cambridge, Hertfordshire, and Bedfordshire — built on the values of environmental responsibility, reliability, and genuine care for every customer.

We believe waste removal doesn't have to harm the planet. That's why we prioritise recycling, donating, and reusing materials wherever possible, ensuring minimal landfill impact with every job we do.
